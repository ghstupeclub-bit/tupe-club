Instructions

This simulation demonstrates the effects of smoking and vaping on the lungs.

To use the simulation, follow these steps:

  • Choose either smoking or vaping.
  • Enter the number of cigarettes or vape puffs per day.
  • Click the "Start" button to begin the simulation.
  • Observe the effects on the lungs, including the degradation of lung health and the buildup of tar and nicotine.
