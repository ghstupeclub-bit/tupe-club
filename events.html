Events & Awareness Days

Important dates for tobacco and vaping education

Key Awareness Days

World No Tobacco Day

May 31

A global day to highlight the health risks of tobacco use and advocate for effective policies to reduce consumption.

Great American Smokeout

Third Thursday of November

Encourages smokers to make a plan to quit, or to plan in advance and quit smoking that day.

National Kick Butts Day

March

A youth initiative that empowers students to stand up, speak out, and raise awareness about the dangers of tobacco.

National Lung Cancer Awareness Month

November

Raises awareness about lung cancer, much of which is caused by smoking, and supports prevention and research.

Take Down Tobacco National Day of Action

April

A national day of activism that empowers people to stand up against the tobacco industry.

School-Based TUPE Events

Year-round

Our school club hosts activities, workshops, and awareness campaigns throughout the year.
